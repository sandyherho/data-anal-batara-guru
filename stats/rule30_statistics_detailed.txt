ANALYSIS OVERVIEW
---------------------------------------------
This comprehensive analysis examines the temporal evolution of two fundamental metrics
in Rule 30 cellular automaton dynamics:

1. COMPOSITIONAL ENTROPY (H_comp):
   - Quantifies the mixing of binary states (0/1) in the configuration
   - Calculated as H_comp = h(rho) where h is binary entropy and rho is density
   - Maximum value of 1 bit indicates equal distribution of states

2. INTERFACE DENSITY (Lambda):
   - Measures spatial heterogeneity through nearest-neighbor mismatches
   - Calculated as fraction of edges connecting different states
   - Maximum value of 0.5 for random configurations (not 1.0)
